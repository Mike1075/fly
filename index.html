FLY

Multiplayer Flight Simulator

How to Play

Controls

  • Pitch Up/Down W / S
  • Roll & Turn A / D
  • Fire Missile Space
  • Boost Speed Shift

Objective

  • Shoot down other players to score points
  • Avoid crashing into the ground
  • Climb the leaderboard!

Leaderboard
